About

The University of Technological Intelligence (UTI), is a premier institution dedicated to nurturing the next generation of technological leaders. Founded in 1969, UTI has rapidly established itself as a global hub for cutting-edge research, innovative programs, and a strong commitment to ethical and responsible technological advancement.

Our mission is to foster a culture of innovation, critical thinking, and collaboration, empowering our students to become agents of positive change in the world. We offer a diverse range of undergraduate and graduate programs across various fields of technology, including computer science, engineering, artificial intelligence, and data science.

UTI's faculty are renowned experts in their respective fields, bringing their passion and expertise to the classroom. They are dedicated to providing students with a rigorous academic experience that challenges them to reach their full potential. Our state-of-the-art facilities and research laboratories offer students ample opportunities to explore their interests, conduct groundbreaking research, and develop practical skills.

Beyond academics, UTI is committed to fostering a vibrant campus community. Students have access to a wide range of extracurricular activities, including clubs, organizations, and athletic teams. Our emphasis on holistic development ensures that our graduates are not only academically accomplished but also well-rounded individuals who can thrive in a rapidly evolving technological landscape.

At UTI, we believe that technology should be used to improve the lives of people and communities around the world. Our research initiatives focus on addressing pressing global challenges, such as climate change, healthcare, and social inequality. We are committed to developing ethical and sustainable technological solutions that benefit society as a whole.

Join the University of Technological Intelligence and be part of a legacy of innovation and excellence.

Vision

We envision a better life for Filipinos by empowering our students with the best globally competitive technological education in engineering, computing, and allied disciplines.

Mission

Through digitalization and innovation in academic design and delivery, U.T.I. students, faculty, staff, and industry partners work together in both traditional and online/flexible learning to transform our students to achieve optimal student outcomes:

Full competence in their fields of study Ability to apply their competencies, mindsets, and values to serve the needs of industry or to pursue technopreneurship to solve societal problems.
