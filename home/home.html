Programs Offered

Undergraduate Programs

Bachelor of Science in Information Technology

Focuses on systems administration, network security, and database management, preparing students for a career in IT.

Bachelor of Science in Computer Science

Emphasizes programming, algorithms, and software development, providing a solid foundation for various tech careers.

Bachelor of Science in Cybersecurity

Dedicated to protecting systems and networks, this program covers ethical hacking, risk management, and security protocols.

Bachelor of Science in Data Science

Combines statistics, machine learning, and data visualization to prepare students for analyzing complex data sets.

Bachelor of Science in Software Engineering

Focuses on advanced software development methodologies and project management, preparing students for leadership roles in technology.

Bachelor of Science in Cloud Computing

FPrepares students to design, implement, and manage cloud-based solutions, focusing on emerging technologies and services.

Graduate Programs

Master of Science in Data Science

Provides advanced knowledge in data analysis, machine learning, and data-driven decision-making for a competitive edge in the industry.

Master of Science in Information Systems

Explores the integration of technology and business processes, focusing on system design, implementation, and management.

Master of Science in Cybersecurity

Advances knowledge of cybersecurity strategies, risk management, and compliance to protect organizational assets.

Master of Science in Artificial Intelligence

Focuses on the development and application of AI technologies, including machine learning, neural networks, and robotics.
